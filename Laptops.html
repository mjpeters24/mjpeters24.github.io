My Favorite (and Unfavorite) Laptops So Far

I've had a couple laptops throughout the years, either hand me downs or one's I bought on my own. Here's some help on deciding which you might want!

  • Lenovo Yoga C940

    • Personally this one is my favorite as it's just been a super dependable, lightweight, decently powerful, cheap laptop. I got it used for dirt cheap and it's 16GB of ram and hefty processor have been carrying me through college with ease. It's been easy to repair so far as I did by it used.

  • Asus VivoBook Flip 12

    • I hate this one. Even though it was free, it just sucked. Slow, bad battery life, and a touchscreen that barely worked. Dont touch it.

  • HP Victus 15

    • Nothing really bad to say about this one. It's meant to be some fancy gaming laptop but I just used it for 3D modeling as the Lenovo wasn't strong enough. Pretty solid laptop, just kinda heavy, large, and to "techy" looking.
